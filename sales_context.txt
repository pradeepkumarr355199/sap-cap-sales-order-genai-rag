Sales Order Processing Policy - SAP SD Context

1. Discount Policy
• If net value exceeds 10,000 EUR, apply a 5% discount automatically.
• Discount approval is required if discount exceeds 10%.
• Bulk quantity orders above 100 units require manager approval.

2. Regional Tax Rules
• Orders from Germany must include VAT at 19%.
• Exports outside EU are tax-exempt with proper documentation.
• VAT ID validation is mandatory for EU B2B customers.

3. Risk and Compliance Rules
• Orders above 50,000 EUR are flagged as high-value risk.
• Customers with overdue invoices may be blocked for new orders.
• Frequent returns may trigger risk review.

4. General Sales Order Context
Sales Orders in SAP SD consist of header and item data. Header contains customer, order type,
and total value. Items contain material, quantity, and pricing details. Net value is calculated as
quantity multiplied by price per unit.
